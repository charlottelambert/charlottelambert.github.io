Example Teaching Materials

This page contains some example course materials I have developed at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

CS 598: Online Moderation
  • Modeling: students build an ML model to predict whether a comment has been removed by moderators. Students will train and consider the tradeoffs between global models across Reddit communities and local models for specific communities.
  • System Development: students will build a simple browser extension to highlight potentially high quality posts and blur potentially low quality posts.
  • Data Analysis: students utilize causal inference methods (e.g., control and treatment group matching, Difference-in-Differences regression analysis) to identify the causal impact of receiving positive feedback on Reddit. This is a strongly scaffolded assignment aimed to teach students the methods as they work.
