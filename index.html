Ongoing Projects:
  • Conversation-Level Resilience: The pervasive nature of antisocial behavior in online communities raises the question: what makes a conversation resilient to the influence of bad actors? I am exploring what types of Reddit conversations are able to withstand an antisocial interruption without turning antisocial and whether the presence of prosocial behavior prior to an interruption has any effect on the resilience of a conversation.
